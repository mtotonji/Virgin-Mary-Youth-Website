Our Mission

Rooted in Christ. Growing Together.

Virgin Mary Youth is a home for Syriac Orthodox youth to grow in faith, build real friendships, and stay connected to Christ and the life of the Church. We are a community where young people can come as they are, find support, and grow together through prayer, fellowship, and shared experiences.

Our mission is to help the next generation live their faith with joy, confidence, and purpose while staying rooted in the traditions of the Syriac Orthodox Church. Whether you are joining for the first time or have been part of the community for years, you have a place here.

Who We Are

About Virgin Mary Youth

A community built on faith, friendship, and a shared calling to serve - welcoming every young heart with open arms.

Our Vision

Building a Generation Rooted in Faith

Virgin Mary Youth is a community built to connect, inspire, and uplift young people through faith, fellowship, and involvement. Our mission is to create a welcoming space where youth can grow spiritually, build lasting friendships, and stay actively connected to the Church through events, service, and meaningful experiences.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

🕊️

Deep Faith

We center everything on a living relationship with God - through prayer, the Sacraments, and Scripture that speaks to real life.

🤝

Genuine Friendship

We create a warm, safe space where every person is valued, heard, and loved - the kind of friendships that last beyond youth group.

🌍

Meaningful Service

Faith without action is incomplete. We actively engage in service projects, outreach, and charitable initiatives.

✨

Joyful Living

Being Christian should be joyful! We celebrate, laugh, eat, and enjoy life together.

🌱

Personal Growth

We help young people discover their gifts and develop into confident, compassionate leaders in the Church and the world.

🫂

Radical Inclusion

All are welcome. No matter your background, questions, or doubts - there is always a place in our hearts.
